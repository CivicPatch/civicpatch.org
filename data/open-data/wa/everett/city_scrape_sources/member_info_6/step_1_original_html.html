Paula Rhyne - District 2

Paula Rhyne was elected to the City Council on Nov. 2, 2021 to represent District 2. Councilmember Rhyne co-chairs the council’s Health and Human Services Committee which covers policy areas that focus on housing affordability, homelessness, public health, and emergency management. She also serves on the council’s Parks and Quality of Life Committee which includes policy areas covering arts, culture, events, and economic development. Councilmember Rhyne serves as a council liaison to the City’s Historic Commission and the Mayor’s Drug Taskforce, the Puget Sound Regional Council’s Economic Development District, and will Chair the City’s Lodging Tax Advisory Committee. In a broader capacity, Councilmember Rhyne was appointed to a third term on the Human Development Committee for the National League of Cities where she takes the stories and perspectives from residents of Everett to help inform policy on a federal level. 

Council Committee co-chair appointments:

  • Health and Human Services: 1st Wednesday, 5 p.m.
  • Budget: Semi-quarterly, 5th Wednesday, 5 p.m.
Council liaison assignments and mayoral appointments:
  • Historical Commission – 4th Tuesday
  • LTAC Chair – Scheduled Annually
  • Mayors Drug Crisis Task Force - twice per month, 2:30 p.m. to 4:30 p.m.
  • National League of Cities Human Development Committee
  • PSRC Economic Development District Board – Quarterly 1st Wed 10am-12pm
