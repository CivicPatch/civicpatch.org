Agendas

To obtain a current Council meeting agenda, meeting minutes or the full packet you can click on the event calendar for the date of the council meeting or email the Executive office or call at 360-403-3441. City Council minutes from 1903 to 2009 are available on the State Archive website.

Note that the packet for the upcoming council meeting is available typically the Thursday afternoon prior to the meeting. The agenda packet shown is the most current available and may be for the meeting that just occurred.
